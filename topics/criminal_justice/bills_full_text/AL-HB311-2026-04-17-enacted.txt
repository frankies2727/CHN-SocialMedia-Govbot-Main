SYNOPSIS:

Under existing law, sheriffs are not authorized

to participate in both a supernumerary program and the

Employees' Retirement System.

This bill would authorize sheriffs who

independently qualify for a supernumerary program and

the Employees' Retirement System based on separate

years of service to participate in both programs.

A BILL

TO BE ENTITLED

AN ACT

Relating to retirement benefits; to authorize

qualifying sheriffs to participate in both a supernumerary

sheriff program and the Employees' Retirement System.

BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF ALABAMA:

Section 1. (a) No individual may participate in both a

supernumerary sheriff program and the Employees' Retirement

System based on the same service.

(b) An individual who independently qualifies for a

supernumerary sheriff program and the Employees' Retirement

System based on separate years of service may participate in

both programs.

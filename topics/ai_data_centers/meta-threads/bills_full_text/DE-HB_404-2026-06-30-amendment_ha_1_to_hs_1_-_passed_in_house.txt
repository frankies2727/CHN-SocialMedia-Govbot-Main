HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
153rd GENERAL ASSEMBLY
HOUSE AMENDMENT NO. 1
TO
HOUSE SUBSTITUTE NO. 1
FOR
HOUSE BILL NO. 404

AMEND House Substitute No. 1 for House Bill No. 404 on line 115 by deleting the date “September 30, 2030” as
it appears therein and inserting in lieu thereof the date “September 30, 2031”.
FURTHER AMEND House Substitute No. 1 for House Bill No. 404 by inserting after line 117 and before line 118
the following accordingly:

“Section 2. This Act shall be effective immediately and implemented July 1, 2027.”

FURTHER AMEND House Substitute No. 1 for House Bill No. 404 by deleting lines 118 and 119 and inserting in

lieu thereof the following:
“Section 3. This Act expires 3 years after the end of the 2027-2028 school year, unless extended by subsequent
action of the General Assembly.”
SYNOPSIS
This amendment clarifies this Act expires 3 years after the end of the 2027-2028 school year unless extended by a
subsequent action of the General Assembly.

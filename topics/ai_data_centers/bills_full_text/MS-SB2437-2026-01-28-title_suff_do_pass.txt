SENATE BILL NO. 2437

AN ACT TO DEFINE "ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE"; AND FOR RELATED
PURPOSES.

BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI:

SECTION 1.

"Artificial intelligence" means a machine-based

system that can, for a given set of human-defined objectives, make

predictions, recommendations or decisions influencing real or

virtual environments.

SYNOPSIS
House Amendment No. 2 to Senate Substitute No. 2 for Senate Bill 19 makes the following changes:
1. Removes “registered agent” from the definition of “resident.”
2. For reciprocal recognition, removes the requirement that the Commissioner determine the regulatory framework
of the other state is substantially similar to those under the Act and removes the requirement that the other state’s
framework have materially equivalent consumer protections.
3. Removes § 3514, which required registration of federally supervised entities and makes conforming changes
throughout.

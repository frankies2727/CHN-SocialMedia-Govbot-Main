EXPLANATION
BY THE LEGISLATIVE COUNCIL
OF
AN ACT
RELATING TO TAXATION -- PROPERTY SUBJECT TO TAXATION

***

This act would repeal the Tiverton tax credit for totally disabled veterans on their real or

personal property. This act would also add the repealed Tiverton tax credit to totally disabled

veterans and extend it to include the veteran's unmarried widow or widower in the amount of four

hundred dollars ($400) or greater.

This act would take effect upon passage.

House Joint Resolution

A joint resolution proposing amendments to Sections 4,

6, and 9 of Article VII and the creation of a new

section in Article XII of the State Constitution to

revise the limitation on annual assessment increases

for specified real property, to increase the homestead

exemption, to provide a limitation on the use of ad

valorem taxes levied by counties and municipalities,

to provide an effective date, and to provide for the

creation of a trust fund for specified purposes.

Be It Resolved by the Legislature of the State of Florida:

That the following amendments to Sections 4, 6, and 9 of

Article VII of the State Constitution and the creation of a new

section in Article XII are agreed to and shall be submitted to

the electors of this state for approval or rejection at the next

general election or at an earlier special election specifically

authorized by law for that purpose:

HOUSE OF REPRESENTATIVES
153rd GENERAL ASSEMBLY
HOUSE AMENDMENT NO. 1
TO
SENATE BILL NO. 306

AMEND Senate Bill No. 306 by deleting lines 31 through 81 in their entirety and by redesignating accordingly.
SYNOPSIS
This Amendment removes candidate qualifications, and the requirement for candidates to file an affidavit attesting
that the candidate meets the qualifications, that Senate Bill No. 306 adds to the Charter of the City of Rehoboth Beach.

SPONSOR: Sen. Townsend

DELAWARE STATE SENATE
153rd GENERAL ASSEMBLY
SENATE AMENDMENT NO. 1
TO
SENATE BILL NO. 233

AMEND Senate Bill No. 233 by deleting lines 21 through 22 in their entirety and inserting in lieu thereof the
following:
“(g) This section does not apply to any of the following:

(1) A person operating a vehicle on a roadway with a speed limit of 25 miles per hour or less.

(2) A farm tractor, as defined in § 101 of this title.

(3) A vehicle that is registered as a farm vehicle exempt from inspection pursuant to § 2113 of this title.”.
SYNOPSIS
This Amendment to Senate Bill No. 233 exempts farm tractors and farm vehicles from the requirements of the
Act. “Farm tractor,” in this context, has the same definition as that term is defined in § 101 of Title 21 and includes motor
vehicles designed and used primarily as a farm implement for drawing plows, mowing machines, and other implements of
husbandry. “Farm vehicle” means a vehicle registered as a farm vehicle exempt from inspection pursuant to § 2113 of Title
21. “Farm tractor” and “farm vehicle” do not include vehicles that are registered as farm trucks, semitrailers, or trailers
pursuant to § 2113 of Title 21; such vehicles are not exempt from the requirements of the Act.

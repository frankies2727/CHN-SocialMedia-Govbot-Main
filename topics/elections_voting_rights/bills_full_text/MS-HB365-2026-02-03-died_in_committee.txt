AN ACT TO CREATE THE EARLY VOTING ACT; TO PROVIDE
DEFINITIONS; TO PROVIDE THE TIME FOR THE EARLY VOTING PERIOD; TO
PROVIDE THAT EARLY VOTING SHALL BE FOR EACH PRIMARY, GENERAL,
RUNOFF, SPECIAL AND MUNICIPAL ELECTION FOR PUBLIC OFFICE; TO
PROVIDE THE HOURS FOR EARLY VOTING IN THE REGISTRAR'S OFFICE AND

DAYS BEFORE THE VOTING BEGINS; TO PROVIDE THE PROCEDURES TO FOLLOW
WHEN CASTING A BALLOT DURING THE EARLY VOTING PERIOD; TO PROVIDE
THE MANNER FOR CHALLENGING THE QUALIFICATIONS OF A VOTER DURING

ARE ELIGIBLE TO VOTE BY ABSENTEE BALLOT; TO PROHIBIT A VOTER FROM
CASTING AN ABSENTEE BALLOT IN PERSON AT THE OFFICE OF THE COUNTY
REGISTRAR; TO CONFORM TO THE PROVISIONS OF THIS ACT; TO AMEND
SECTIONS 23-15-31, 23-15-37, 23-15-43, 23-15-47, 23-15-65,
23-15-127, 23-15-153, 23-15-171, 23-15-173, 23-15-191, 23-15-195,

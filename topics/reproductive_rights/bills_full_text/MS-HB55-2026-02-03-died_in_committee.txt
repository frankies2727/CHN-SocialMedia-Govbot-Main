HOUSE BILL NO.

AN ACT TO AMEND SECTION 97-3-5, MISSISSIPPI CODE OF 1972, TO
PROHIBIT THE MANUFACTURE OR MAIL ORDER IN THIS STATE OF MEDICINES
THAT CAUSE ANY ABORTION THAT IS PROHIBITED BY THE LAWS OF THIS
STATE; AND FOR RELATED PURPOSES.

BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF MISSISSIPPI:

SECTION 1.

amended as follows:

97-3-5.

Section 97-3-5, Mississippi Code of 1972, is

distributes, transports, delivers, sells, lends, gives away, or in

in his or her possession with intent to manufacture, market, mail,

distribute, transport, deliver, sell, lend, or give away, or

or article, or any drug or medicine, for causing * * * an abortion

that is prohibited in this state; or who writes or prints, or

causes to be written or printed, a card, circular, pamphlet,

advertisement, or notice of any kind, or gives information orally,

stating when, where, how, of whom, or by what means such

AMEND Senate Substitute No. 1 for Senate Bill No. 278 on line 25 by deleting “authorize” and inserting in lieu
thereof “pre-authorize”.
FURTHER Amend Senate Substitute No. 1 for Senate Bill No. 278 on lines 26 and 31 by deleting “authorized”
each time it appears and inserting in lieu thereof “pre-authorized”.
FURTHER Amend Senate Substitute No. 1 for Senate Bill No. 278 on lines 26, 28, and 32 by deleting
“authorization” each time it appears and inserting in lieu thereof “pre-authorization”.
SYNOPSIS
This Amendment clarifies that the earlier authorizations necessary for summer enrollment are pre-authorizations
and still subject to meeting eligibility requirements for full authorization at the time the child will attend.

HOUSE BILL No. 2542
By Representatives Long and Pishny
1-26

AN ACT concerning roads and highways; designating a portion of United
States highway 56 as the Bill Tucker memorial highway.
Be it enacted by the Legislature of the State of Kansas:
Section 1. The portion of United States highway 56 in Morton county
from its junction with county road 14, then northeast to its junction with
county road 16 is hereby designated as the Bill Tucker memorial highway.
Upon compliance with K.S.A. 68-10,114, and amendments thereto, the
secretary of transportation shall place highway signs along the highway
right-of-way at proper intervals to indicate that the highway is the Bill
Tucker memorial highway.
Sec. 2. This act shall take effect and be in force from and after its
publication in the statute book Kansas register.
